Full Time Missionary Accommodations

Our cozy suites are designed to be a perfect haven for full time temple missionaries. They are located within walking distance to the temple and each room comes with everything for a comfortable long term stay, kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, and living room. You will also have access to all of our other amenities such as our breakfast, swimming pool, and family history search room. If you have any other questions about our long term stay rooms please feel free to contact us.
